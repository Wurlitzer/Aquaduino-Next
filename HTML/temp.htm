Temperature turning on the associated actor of this controller
When the actor supports PWM this is the temperature when the actuator reaches its maximum.
The PWM is increased linear inbetween the threshold and the the maximum.
The actuator is turned off when the temperature drops below threshold - hysteresis
This prevents the actuator from being turned on shortly after it has been turned out.
